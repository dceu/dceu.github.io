## Backslash Escapes
Markdown provides backslash escapes for the following characters:
 * \\ backslash  
 * \` backtick  
 * \* asterisk  
 * \_ underscore  
 * \{\} curly braces  
 * \[\] square brackets  
 * \(\) parenthesis  
 * \# hashmark  
 * \+ plus sign  
 * \- minus sign(hyphen)  
 * \. dot  
 * \! exclamation mark
